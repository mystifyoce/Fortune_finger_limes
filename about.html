Benifits of Finger Limes

Finger limes were used by the indigenous people for many things for thousands of years. The antibacterial properties in the pulp and juice of the finger limes worked as an antiseptic and were applied on cuts and sores to help inflammation and infection. The indigenous people trusted this as a medicine as well as a healthy food group that supplied energy. Finger limes have several health benefits ranging from vitamin B, vitamin C and vitamin E. Finger limes are also a great source of potassium and antioxidant. Enriched in each fruit, vitamins are necessary for your bodies daily functions. Just like any healthy person it's good to take note of what you are putting into your body and seeing the results from these healthy foods.

Vitamin E promotes eye health and prevents inflammation, Red champagne finger limes contain exceptionally high levels of vitamin E. The Vitamin C enriched in finger limes contains almost three times the amount of vitamin C as a mandarin. It is also necessary for growth, development and the repairing of body tissues, furthermore it assists in forming collagen and promotes iron absorption for the immune system.

Vitamin B is used in the production of red blood cells, which is especially important for young expectant mothers. This helps with the growth and development of the baby's brain and spinal cord.

Potassium and antioxidants Potassium benefits include the lowering of blood pressure, helps with muscle contraction and also helps with disease prevention such as kidney stones, osteoporosis and lowers the risk of strokes. Finally, Antioxidants. This helps with weight loss and contributes to a healthier you.
